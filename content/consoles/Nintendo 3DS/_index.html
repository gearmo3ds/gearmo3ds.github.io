But What is Handheld Music?

The term "Handheld Music" refers to the incredibly (and unfortunately) small number of programs released over the years by various game studios and indivuals that aim to help the user create music on their gaming handhelds.

Game Boy

LSDJ and Nanoloop pioneered chiptune composition on the go.

Nintendo DS

Apps like the KORG DS-10 and NitroTracker brought full synths and samplers.

Nintendo 3DS

The KORG M01D and Rytmik Ultimate games expanded handheld sound design.

Nintendo Switch

Apps like KORG Gadget and Rytmik Studio continue the tradition.
